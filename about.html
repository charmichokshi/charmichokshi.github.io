Google Developers Group Ahmedabad is for developers who are interested in Google's developer technology; everything from the Android, App Engine, and Google Chrome platforms, to product APIs like the Maps API, YouTube API and Google Calendar API. We organise TechTalks, Hackathons, Tech Seminars around Google developers technologies and open-source to encourage knowledge sharing.
