GDG Ahmedabad

Google Developers Group Ahmedabad, founded on Aug 16, 2015 is for the developers keen towards Google's developer technology; everything from the Android and Google Chrome platforms, to product APIs like the Maps, YouTube and Google Calendar. GDG has been engaged into organizing TechTalks, Hackathons and Tech Seminars around Google developer technologies. GDG is a proud host to 4000+ members with 30+ meetups organized over the span of its establishment and the credit goes to the incredible partisans- the 7 organizers of GDG Ahmedabad that have been working relentlessly for the developer community to flourish.
